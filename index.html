Why is renting in NYC so expensive?

Most landlords in New York City strictly follow the 40x rule: your annual pre-tax income must be at least 40 times the monthly rent. If you don't meet this, you'll likely need a guarantor who earns 80x the rent.

The Hidden Costs of 2026 NYC Rentals

  • Broker Fees: Even in 2026, most prime Manhattan apartments require a fee (typically 12-15% of the annual rent).
  • Security Laws: Remember, NY state law limits security deposits to exactly one month's rent.
  • Application Fees: It is illegal for landlords to charge more than $20 for background checks and credit reports.
